# Task 4 – Data Visualization (Global Superstore)

## Project Overview
This task focuses on creating an interactive Power BI dashboard using the Global Superstore dataset.  
The goal was to analyze sales and profit performance across different years, categories, and regions, and present the findings in a clear and simple way.

---

## Dashboard Details
The dashboard includes:

- Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, and Profit Margin  
- Sales by Category  
- Monthly Sales Trend  
- Profit by Sub-Category  
- Sales by Country  
- Filters for Year, Segment, and Country  
- A separate page summarizing key insights and observations  

---

## Files Included
- **PBIX File** – Complete Power BI dashboard  
- **PDF Export** – Dashboard and summary for review  
- **Dataset (Excel)** – Global Superstore data used for analysis  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales across 2012–2015 total around **$12.64M** with a profit of **$1.47M**.  
- **Technology** contributes the highest sales among categories.  
- Sub-categories like **Copiers and Phones** show strong profitability.  
- The **United States** leads in total sales by a large margin.  
- A noticeable increase in sales occurs during **October to December**.  
- Some sub-categories (such as Binders and Storage) show weaker profit performance.

---

## Recommendations
- Focus more on high-profit items to improve overall profitability.  
- Revisit pricing or discount strategies for low-performing sub-categories.  
- Strengthen marketing efforts in regions outside the US to balance sales distribution.  
- Plan promotional activities ahead of the year-end period, where demand is higher.
